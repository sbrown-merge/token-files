# Secondary Colors
These colors should be used sparingly — only when more colors are necessary. Look for opportunies to add the **Electrolight Green** but be aware that contrast on a white background fails WCAG accessibility testing. It does pass when used on a Dark Green background. This color can also be used for any graphic accents that aren't a necessity to content comprehension. Medium Gray and Black are used for text colors. Avoid using Black as a flood color.

# Tertiary Colors
In circumstances where more than five colors are needed to differentiate data or information — such as charts and graphs — these tertiary colors can be used.

Charts and graphs that require less than five colors to differentiate the information should use only the primary and secondary palettes.
